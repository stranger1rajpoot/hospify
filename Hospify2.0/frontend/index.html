Core Features

Everything Your Hospital Needs

A complete, end-to-end hospital management solution covering every department and workflow.

Multi-Tenant Architecture

Manage multiple hospitals from one platform. Each hospital has isolated data with a shared system. Super Admin controls all, Hospital Admin controls their own.

JWT & Role-Based Security

Secure JWT authentication, bcrypt password hashing, and granular role-based access control for 9 different user types across all modules.

OPD / IPD Management

Complete outpatient and inpatient workflow — token queue, appointment booking, ward management, bed assignment, and discharge summaries.

Digital Prescriptions

Doctors create digital prescriptions with medicines, dosage, frequency, and duration. Auto-sync with pharmacy for dispensing. PDF export supported.

Lab Management

Full laboratory module — test requests, sample tracking, result entry, report upload, and integrated billing. Priority levels: Routine, Urgent, STAT.

Pharmacy & Inventory

Real-time medicine stock tracking, expiry alerts, low-stock warnings, barcode support, and integrated pharmacy billing with prescription sync.

Smart Billing & Invoicing

Generate detailed invoices with automatic tax calculations. Supports credit cards, mobile wallets, and cash, tracking pending balances and payment status.

Analytics Dashboard

Real-time revenue analytics, patient growth charts, OPD/IPD statistics, staff metrics, pharmacy income, and appointment analytics with Chart.js.

Notification System

Integrated in-app notifications, SMS reminders, and email alerts. Keep patients updated on appointment slots, lab reports, and invoice dues.
